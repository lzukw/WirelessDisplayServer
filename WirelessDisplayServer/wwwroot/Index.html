Wireless Display

Stream the screen-contents of the presentation-computer via WLAN to the projecting-computer, which then displays the screen-contents using the projector.

Prerequisites

Either VNC or FFmpeg is used for streaming. Therefore, on the presentation-computer either a VNC-server, or ffmpeg must be installed.

VNC is very efficient and suitable for WLAN with low-bandwidth. However, changes of large areas of the screen can take some seconds. On the other hand, FFmpeg produces much network-traffic, but has low latency.
